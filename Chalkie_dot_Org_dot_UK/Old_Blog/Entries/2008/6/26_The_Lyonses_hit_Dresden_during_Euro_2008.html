Lyonses and Hooligans

 

The Lyonses hit Dresden during Euro 2008

 

Euro 2008 is upon us, and Germany are doing pretty well. Let’s not mention England - cough. Abby and Dave came to visit with their scooter racing tots in tow, and we hung out with Dave’s brother Keith and his wife Martina, and Grandma Lyons and Gwen (Keith also lives here in Dresden, after coming back from China to be with the wife and kid. I met them all first when we all lived in Rochester MN, and played in bands). One evening we were in the park with the kids while a game was on, and it was all very quiet, not a soul in sight... all watching the game on TV.


Well, quiet until germany scored, then a very odd sound made us all jump. Several thousand people indoors making the same cheering noise at the exact same time makes for unusually startling listening outside.

Thursday, June 26, 2008

Later on in the evening of the semi final, I was back home watching the celebrations outside my window on the infamous corner of Louisenstrasse/Gorlitzerstrasse, where all the randoms always hang out. The roads were impassable, filled with revelers, just like being back during the BRN street festival again. To say it was a bit noisy would be something of an understatement. In fact after some hours it degenerated into a shouting and chanting circle of hooligans throwing bottles into the middle and dancing on the broken glass, then throwing fireworks into it all. Football hooligans are as brainless as each other all over the globe it seems.


...anyhoooo, it was great to see Abby and Dave and the wee ‘uns and I had much fun helping wrangle them and getting them covered head to foot in chocolate ice cream etc. Grandma Diane and Keith were as entertaining as ever, and Abby and Dave brought me much needed supplies such as salt and vinegar crisps, Branston pickle, scottish sweets and many other goodies. Must find time to get up to Jockland and visit Abby at theirs. Last time I went was for their wedding, where I lost my first ever mobile phone... but that’s another story.
