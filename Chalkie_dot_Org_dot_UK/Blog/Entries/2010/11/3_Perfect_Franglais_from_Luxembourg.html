Perfect Franglais from Luxembourg

 

It’s hard to read... so from the Hotel Am Klouschter room card:

    “By respect for nature, reuse the linen of the toilet several times. Alone the linen of toilet deposited in the shower will be given in washing.”


Reverse google translated back into French gives:

    “Par respect pour la nature, la réutilisation du linge de la toilette à plusieurs reprises. Seul le linge de toilette déposé dans la douche sera donnée à laver.”


By some modern miracle, this is only slightly different from the French text on the card... suggesting google translate was used  for the english translation. Thats just cheating!

Well, it amused me for a couple of minutes.
